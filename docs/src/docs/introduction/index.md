# Introduction

Comaney is a budgeting app that helps you answer two simple questions:

- **Where is my money going?**
- **How much do I have left to spend this month?**

You record your income, your bills, and your day-to-day spending, and Comaney shows you the full picture. There are charts, totals, and reminders, but nothing you have to configure before you can get started.

## How Comaney thinks about money

Every financial month starts fresh. Comaney adds up your income, subtracts your spending, and tells you what is left. There are four types of transaction:

| Type | What it means |
|---|---|
| **Expense** | Money you spend: groceries, rent, a coffee. |
| **Income** | Money you receive: salary, a refund, a gift. |
| **Savings deposit** | Money you move into savings. Treated like spending because it leaves your spending budget. |
| **Savings withdrawal** | Money you take back from savings. Treated like income because it returns to your budget. |

A fifth type, **carry-over**, is created automatically if you configure Comaney to roll unused budget from one month into the next. You do not create carry-overs yourself.
