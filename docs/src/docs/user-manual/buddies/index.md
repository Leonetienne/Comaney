# Buddies

Buddies lets you track shared spending with other people. Whether you are splitting a restaurant bill with one friend, covering each other's grocery runs, or going on a group holiday, Comaney keeps a running tally of who owes who so you never have to work it out yourself.

## How it works

Whoever pays upfront logs the full expense and marks which people share the cost. Each person's share is recorded automatically. Everyone's balance stays up to date.

## Two ways to share

**One-on-one buddies** are for splitting costs with a single person. You can add someone as an offline buddy (they do not need a Comaney account) or connect with another Comaney user by sending them an invitation.

**Groups** are for three or more people sharing a pool of expenses together. A group has a shared expense list and a breakdown of who owes who across all costs. Perfect for a shared flat, a holiday, or any situation with multiple people splitting multiple bills.
