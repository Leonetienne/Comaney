# Adding Buddies

Go to **Buddies** in the navigation, then **My Buddies** to manage all your connections.

## Offline buddy

An offline buddy is someone who does not use Comaney. You just give them a name. Only you can see the balance and only you can track expenses for them. Comaney cannot contact them automatically.

**To add one:**

1. Go to **My Buddies**.
2. Under **Add an offline buddy**, type a name and click **Add**.

The buddy appears in your list straight away.

**Want to link their Comaney account later?** If the person eventually signs up, you can connect their account to the existing entry so the full expense history carries over. See [Upgrading an offline buddy](#upgrading-an-offline-buddy-to-a-connected-user) below.

## Inviting another Comaney user

For one-on-one expense sharing with someone who already has (or will create) a Comaney account.

**To send an invitation:**

1. Go to **My Buddies**.
2. Under **Invite a buddy**, enter their email address and click **Send invite**.

They receive an email with a link. Once they click **Accept**, you are connected and can share expenses.

- The invitation expires after 7 days.
- You can revoke a pending invitation at any time from **My Buddies** under "Buddy invitations you sent".
- If the email address is not yet registered, Comaney sends them a sign-up link instead. They are automatically linked to you once they create their account.

**Receiving an invitation:** If someone invites you, you will see it under **Pending Invitations** on the My Buddies page. Click **Accept** to connect or **Decline** to ignore it.

## Creating a group

Groups are for three or more people sharing a pool of expenses. One person creates the group and becomes the admin.

**To create a group:**

1. Go to **My Buddies**.
2. Under **Create a group**, type a name and click **Create**.
3. You land on the group page. From here, invite members and start logging expenses.

## Inviting people to a group

Only the group **admin** can invite members.

1. On the group detail page, find the **Invite a member** form.
2. Enter their email address and click **Invite**.

The person receives an email with a link to join. Once they accept, they appear in the member list and both of you become connected as one-on-one buddies as well.

- If the email is not yet registered, Comaney sends them a sign-up and group-join link.
- Pending invitations are listed on the group page and can be revoked there.

**Receiving a group invitation:** Go to **My Buddies**. The invitation appears under **Pending Invitations**. Click **View invite** to accept or decline.

## Adding an offline member to a group

If someone in your group does not use Comaney, you can add them as an offline member.

1. On the group detail page, find **Add an offline member**, enter a name, and click **Add**.

Their name appears in the member list. You can log expenses on their behalf and settle their share yourself. You can also invite them to join Comaney later and link their account to the offline entry (see below).

## Upgrading an offline buddy to a connected user

Once an offline buddy has a Comaney account, you can link it to their existing entry so the full shared expense history moves to their real account.

**For a personal offline buddy:**

1. On **My Buddies**, find the offline buddy and click **Invite as user**.
2. Enter their email address and click **Send**.

**For an offline group member** (admin only):

1. On the group detail page, find the offline member and click **Invite to merge**.
2. Enter their email address and click **Send**.

In both cases, they receive an email. When they accept the link in that email, their account is connected, all expense history transfers over, and the offline entry is replaced by a real connection.
