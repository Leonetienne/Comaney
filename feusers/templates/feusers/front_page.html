01 — Expense tracking

Every cent,
accounted for.

Log income and expenses with a title, value, due date, payee, and a note. Assign categories and tags to each entry and Comaney tells you exactly where your money went at the end of every month.

  • Categories and tags on every entry
  • Income and expenses side by side
  • Mark entries settled as you pay them
  • Payee and note fields for full context
02 — AI Express Creation

Describe it.
Done.

Type what you spent in plain language or snap a picture of your receipt and the AI turns it into properly categorised expense entries. Review the preview, adjust if needed, confirm. No tedious form filling, no manual categorisation. Every new user on the official public instance gets a free monthly budget of ai requests so you can try it within minutes of signing up.

  • Plain language input, no forms to fill
  • Scans your receipt
  • Auto-assigns categories and tags
  • Handles multiple expenses at once
  • Free budget included for every account
  • Use your own API key to remove limits
  • Define default instructions
03 — Scheduled expenses

Recurring bills,
never forgotten.

Define rent, subscriptions, and regular bills once. Comaney generates the entries automatically each financial month. Tick auto-settle and they take care of themselves completely. The less you have to touch it, the better.

  • Weekly, monthly, or custom intervals
  • Auto-generated at the start of each period
  • Auto-settle on due date
  • Never log rent or Netflix manually again
04 — Monthly overview

Always know
where you stand.

At any point in the month Comaney tells you: how much came in, how much went out, what is still outstanding, and exactly how much you have left to spend. It works with your actual pay cycle, not the calendar month, so the numbers always reflect reality.

  • Spending budget and remaining balance, live
  • Set your own financial month start day
  • Breakdown by category and tag
  • Settled vs. outstanding at a glance
05 — Open source

Your data stays
yours.

Comaney can run on your own server. If you host yourself, financial data never touches a third party cloud, never gets sold, and is never used to train anything, unless you use the AI feature which utilizes Anthropics AI services. Export everything as a ZIP of CSVs at any time, and since the code is fully open source, you can read and audit every line of it.

  • 100% self-hostable, no cloud required (without ai)
  • Full export as ZIP and CSV at any time
  • Open source on GitHub
  • No tracking, no ads, no nonsense
